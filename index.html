Program Details

Deep learning since the start of the 21st century has been witnessing a surge and interest similar to that witnessed by quantum mechanics at the start of the 20th century. While in its early days in the last decade, experimentalists have proven its might and we see a rise in practical deployment of deep learning based solutions across vision, language, speech, signal processing, etc. of which some include chat bots with ambient assisted intelligence like Alexa, Siri, Cortana. Although the advent of specialized toolboxes for the trade, and availability of numerous open source models has enabled rapid deployment of commercial solutions, there is a lack in the communityâ€™s in-depth understanding of underlying mathematical foundations which make such technologies so powerful. This has led to the mathematical reasons for its success to remain elusive and ill-understood.

This course will initially start with a mathematical understanding of the objectives and basis of machine learning, traversing across through linear algebra and data structure associated with representing different deep neural networks, bounds of their performance, statistics and information theory required for understanding learning principles and optimization, principles behind generative models and adversarial learning, finally to conclude in the mathematical understanding of explainability for reasoning with a deep neural network.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
Do you provide a certificate?
(Answer) Yes, all attendees to MoDL 2020 would be awarded a certificate of participation issued by Continuing Education Program (CEP), Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur. This certificate is valued at 3 Countinuing Education Units (CEU) following Institute norms.
Are any subsidized price registrations are available?
(Answer) The rates provided include all applicable subsidies.
Is there a final test?
(Answer) We have session wise assessments for the final credit evaluation.
What are the lab sessions based on?
(Answer) Bring in your Laptops for the lab sessions conducted with Python and Pytorch. GPU not necessarily needed for the exercises.
What is included in the registration package?
(Answer) Registration package includes applicable and associated couse material, breakfast, refreshments and lunch served on venue, access to all technical sessions, hands-on lab sessions.
Does the registration package include accommodation?
(Answer) No, we shall not be able to arrange for accommodation, since hostels are not available at this extension centre. You may arrange to stay at the numerous Hotels located in the vicinity of Salt Lake City, Kolkata.
Do you provide TA/DA to attendees?
(Answer) Organizers of this program are currently not able to extend any support of TA/DA to attendees. TEQIP participants to claim TA/DA from their parent institute only.
